Cars 3 

			
						Blindsided by a new generation of blazing-fast racers, the legendary Lightning McQueen is suddenly pushed out of the sport he loves. 
						To get back in the game, he will need the help of an eager young race technician with her own plan to win, inspiration from the late 
						Fabulous Hudson Hornet, and a few unexpected turns. Proving that #95 isn't through yet will test the heart of a champion on Piston Cup Racing's biggest stage!

						
						
						Genre:Animation, Comedy, Adventure, Family, Sport
						Stars:Owen Wilson, Tony Shalhoub, Nathan Fillion, Chris Cooper, Armie Hammer, Larry The Cable Guy, Cristela Alonzo, Ray Magliozzi
						Director:Brian Fee
						Country:United States
